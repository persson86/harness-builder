# Voice and Tone

Voice and tone reference for interface copy.

---

## Voice

Voice is stable — it doesn't change by context or situation. It defines how the product communicates with people.

### Principles

**Direct, not blunt:**
Get to the point. Avoid filler and unnecessary technical jargon. But be human — direct doesn't mean harsh.

> ✅ "Your session expired. Sign in again to continue."
>
> ❌ "A situation related to user authentication has occurred that requires re-verification."

**Clear, not condescending:**
Use language anyone understands, without treating the user as if they know nothing.

> ✅ "Couldn't save. Check your connection and try again."
>
> ❌ "A 503 error occurred at the persistence endpoint."

**Helpful, not evasive:**
Offer the next step whenever possible. Avoid messages that only identify the problem without helping to resolve it.

> ✅ "File too large. The limit is 10 MB — compress the file or use a different format."
>
> ❌ "Invalid file."

---

## Tone

Tone varies by context. Use the table below to calibrate copy for each situation.

| Situation | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding / welcome | Welcoming, encouraging | "Ready to get started? Let's set up your account." |
| Action completed successfully | Positive, concise | "Saved successfully." |
| Recoverable error | Calm, guiding | "Couldn't connect. Try again." |
| Critical error / data loss | Transparent, empathetic | "Something went wrong and your data may not have been saved. Contact support." |
| Destructive action | Neutral, precise | "Delete this project? This action cannot be undone." |
| Empty state | Motivating, instructional | "Nothing here yet. Create the first one to get started." |
| Loading / processing | Informative | "Processing your file…" |
| Tooltip / contextual help | Explanatory, jargon-free | "Card expiry date in MM/YY format." |

---

## What to avoid

- ❌ Blaming the user: "You didn't fill in the field correctly"
- ❌ Passive-aggressive tone: "Please fill in all required fields"
- ❌ Exposed technical jargon: "timeout", "null pointer", "422 Unprocessable Entity"
- ❌ Excessive exclamation: "Congratulations!!!" or "Great job!"
- ❌ Unnecessary negativity: "Unfortunately it was not possible to…"
- ❌ Verbosity: messages longer than 2 sentences without real need
